Sketch 7: Sprite Sheets

The Forest

In this game, the player's objective is to discover and eliminate the enemy who has taken over the forest. Guided by a small, silent fairy, the player can collect various weapons and tools in order to accomplish this mission. The perils and dangers of this dark forest are inspired by those depicted in the original Grimms' fairy tales.


This is the fairy who acts as a guide throughout your quest.
